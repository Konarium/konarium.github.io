1. What Information We Collect

The majority of information we gather from you comes from your interaction with our games or ads displayed within our games or other publishers' games. Additionally, we may collect information you provide us when you voluntarily contact us. We also do not strive to collect any information that can be used to contact or identify you directly, such as your full name, email address, postal address or phone number (“Personally Identifiable Information” or “PII”).

The information we collect can be categorized into three broad categories:

1.1 Information We Collect Directly From You

We may ask you to provide personal information voluntarily in certain parts of our services. This may include details necessary for us to process your inquiries, such as your name and email address, which you may submit to our support team via email. We will explain the personal information you are asked to provide and the reasons why we need it when we request it from you.

When you use our services, you may also directly provide us with personal information, which we will store and process for the purposes described in our privacy policy. This information may include your game username or alias, profile photo or avatar, approximate location, details about the games and platforms you use, links to your social media profiles and ID number, information about your purchases, and personal information you provide when requesting help from our customer support team. If you create an account, we may collect your name, username, email address, password, and other information that helps us confirm your identity. We may collect information visible via your Facebook account if you sign in with Facebook Connect.

If you ask for help from our customer support team, we will collect and store your contact information, information about your game-play or activity, and your username or ID number. We may also collect information about your interests and communication preferences that you choose to provide, such as when subscribing to news or alerts.

Note About Sensitive or Special Categories

As a general practice, we do not ask for or intend to collect sensitive or special categories of information. However, there may be certain circumstances where you may choose to provide us with this information, such as when you contact our Customer Support team for assistance. It's important to note that the definition of sensitive or special categories of information may vary by location and could encompass data such as health records, biometric information, race, religion, political opinions or philosophical beliefs, sexual preferences or orientation, genetic information, or precise location data.

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

As you use our Services, we may collect certain information automatically from you and/or your device. This information may be considered personal information under applicable data protection laws and includes data and analytics about your use of our Services (such as in-game progress, levels, games played, session length, preferred settings, in-game adverts viewed, in-game references, and game preferences), your IP address, and device identifiers like Advertising ID and Device ID numbers. We also collect information about your device type and operating system, in-game communications with other players, and broad geographic location based on your IP address. Advertising ID is a unique identifier used for personalized advertising by advertisers and is consistent across all mobile applications, allowing cross-app tracking. On Apple, you can opt-out of Advertising ID by choosing the "limit ad tracking" option in your privacy settings. On Google Android, you can opt-out by choosing "Opt-out of personalized ads." Device identifier/information includes technical data like screen size, processor, device type, model, maker, and operating system, or a combination of this data available from a device for a developer for checking the device compatibility with a particular application.

We will gather personal information about your use of our Services, including the duration and frequency of gameplay, interactions with other players and game features, purchases made, progress and levels achieved, and other gameplay activities. In order to identify your game account, we may create a player ID. We may keep this content on our servers to enhance our Services, conduct research, manage and deliver advertising, and protect the safety and well-being of our players, as well as our rights and property. Our servers will automatically record certain data about your use of the Services, such as your IP address, device type, operating system, links and advertisements clicked on, and duration of interaction with the Services. This information is analyzed to improve and enhance the Services by expanding their features and functionality and tailoring them to your needs and preferences. We also use this data to generate aggregate, non-identifying information about how the Services are used, verify user eligibility for certain services, and monitor and analyze this data to increase our Services’ functionality and user-friendliness. If required by law, we will seek your consent for using this information.

Cookies and Other Similar Technologies

Cookies are small data files that may contain an anonymous unique identifier. They are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your computer's hard drive. We and our partners use cookies and similar technologies to provide and personalize the Service, analyze usage, target advertisements, and prevent fraud. Cookies allow us and our partners to store preferences and track activities within the Service. It's important to note that our partners may have their own privacy policies.

We and our partners collect and store information about users' interactions with unaffiliated websites and applications that use our technologies, including cookies and similar tracking technologies. This allows us to identify a common user or household behind multiple devices or browsers and link those browsers and devices into a device graph. We do this to detect and prevent fraud, allow users to seamlessly use the Service across multiple devices, provide analytics, personalize content, limit the number of times a user sees the same advertisement across devices, and provide personalized advertising based on browsing patterns across devices.

While you can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or indicate when a cookie is being sent, doing so may limit your ability to use some features of our Service.
